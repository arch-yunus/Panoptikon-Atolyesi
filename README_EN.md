## 🎯 Project Vision
**Panoptikon-Workshop** is an open-source hub dedicated to exploring the philosophical universe built by Michel Foucault. It aims to deconstruct modern truth regimes, digital surveillance apparatuses, and the construction of the subject by synthesizing historical texts with 21st-century technological landscapes.

---

## 🏗️ Repository Architecture

The compendium is categorized systematically to provide researchers with precise context mapping:

### 🔹 [01] Systematics of Concepts
*Foundational building blocks and terminological glossary.*
* **Power-Knowledge Matrix:** Strategic dynamics in truth production.
* **Biopower:** Management of the population as a biological mass.
* **Parrhesia:** The courage of truth and the ethical subject.

### 🔸 [02] Archaeological Excavations
*Deep methodological research into the historical unconscious of knowledge.*
* **The Order of Things:** Epistemological ruptures and the "death of man."
* **The Birth of the Clinic:** The medical gaze and spatialization of the body.

### 🔹 [03] Genealogical Studies
*Micro-physics of power and disciplinary mechanisms over the body.*
* **Discipline and Punish:** Panopticism and the disciplinary society.
* **Governmentality:** From raison d'état to neoliberal arts of governing.

### 🔸 [04] Modern Panopticon & Digital Era
*Applying Foucault's lens to 21st-century technologies.*
* **Algorithmic Domination:** Data mining as new biopolitics.
* **AI & Blockchain:** Automated truth regimes and protocol power.

## 🔬 Methodological Approach
Every analysis in this workshop passes through a triple filter:
1.  **Archaeology:** Excavating the historical conditions that make knowledge possible.
2.  **Genealogy:** Exposing the power struggles at the origin of values and norms.
3.  **Discourse Analysis:** Deconstructing how truth regimes are linguistically constructed.

---
*This workshop is a continuous philosophical construction site intended to make invisible power mechanisms visible.*
